A leading supplier of industrial, commercial, construction and military products, Maverick Industries was founded in 1991. Customers relied on Maverick to supply superior products, on time, and at a competitive price. Their comprehensive range of quality products and large inventory enabled Maverick to develop long lasting business relationships with customers. Since Triad's acquisition of Maverick Industries, Inc. in 2021, we continue to uphold that same commitment to customer service. Our technical expertise and value-added solutions are an exciting complement to Maverick's product offerings. Former Maverick customers can be assured that the availability of products and service levels will remain the same throughout this transition. Explore our site to see the additional products and services available through Triad Technologies. If you have any questions about the acquisition and name change of Maverick Industries, please do not hesitate to contact us.
